Spam Fried Rice

Description

Spam is a tasty, widely available meat that adds an excellent burst of flavor when paired with fried rice.

Ingredients

  • Spam Can
  • Rice
  • Vegetables you like

Steps

  1. Take the spam out of can, and cut into fine, square chunks
  2. Fry the spam chunks until golden-brown
  3. Fry rice with vegetables of choice
  4. Mix the spam chunks with rice and vegetables
  5. Serve and enjoy
