How to make Bulgogi

Description

Bulgogi, or Korean Barbeque, is an amazing dish that upon smelling it, would invoke salivation and high expectations for a tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb of boneless rib eye steak
  • Brown sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Gochujang, Korean red pepper paste
  • Green Onions
  • Sesame seeds
  • Garlic
  • Sesame Oil
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Wrap steak in plastic wrap and put in freezer for 20-30 minutes
  2. Unwrap and slice across grain in 1/4 inch slices
  3. Combine soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, ginger and gochujang in a bowl
  4. Marinate steak and mixture from previous step for 2 hours to overnight
  5. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in frying pan over medium-high heat
  6. Cook steak until charred and cooked through
  7. Serve with rice and kimchi. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds, if desired
